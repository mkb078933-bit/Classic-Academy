About Us

Empowering Your Success with Quality Education

➜

Classic Academy Larkana is a well-established vocational, IT, and coaching institute in Larkana, Pakistan, founded in 1991. It serves as a recognized campus for the Virtual University of Pakistan and offers a wide variety of services including degree programs, short IT courses, and academic coaching. Our academy is built on a simple belief: every student deserves access to great teaching, real-world skills, and a community that supports their growth. From day one, our faculty and staff work closely with each student to help them reach their goals.
